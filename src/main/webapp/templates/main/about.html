About We Technologies

What's all this 2.0 about?


Last year (April, 2014) we gathered as a team for NASA's SpaceApps2014, which was hosted in Zaragoza among others.

Our first approach to the event was really confusing as it was everyone's first time ever participating on a hackaton. We were rather unexperienced with modern technologies, mostly knowing Java, JSP, Matlab and some basic HTML/CSS, in particular CSS was something really diffuse and weird.

So, we developed our first very serious web application ever, named EarthMe!

* * *

Now, one year after, we have learned a lot more on technologies and development so, in commemoration of this event that awoke our spirits on this amazing world, we decided to work on a revamp of EarthMe!
